Macronutrients


  • Protein(4 calories per gram.)-> Proteins carry out a variety of functions like ensuring the growth and development of muscles, bones, hair, and skin, and forming antibodies, hormones, and other essential substances.
  • Carbohydrates(4 calories per gram)-> Carbs fuel your body, especially your central nervous system and brain, and protect against disease.
  • Fat(9 calories per gram.)-> Fat supports many of your body’s functions such as vitamin and mineral absorption, blood clotting, building cells, and muscle movement.

Foods High in Protein- beef, chicken, turkey, cheese, milk, whey protein, eggs, beans, peanuts, fish, shellfish
Foods High in Fats- egg yolks, salmon, cheese, cream, whole milk, yogurt, mayonnaise, nuts, olives, chia seeds
Foods High in Carbs- beans, lentils, oats, wheat, bananas, mangoes, quinoa, potatoes, breads, pastas, baked goods
